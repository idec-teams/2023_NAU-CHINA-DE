      Experiment:

      1.Determine DNA concentration

      (1)Remove the foam board and insert the U disk - select dsDNA - blank detection - determine

      (2) Elution Buffer is used as a blank control, and EB is added again after the blank determine, and the zero condition is determine (as close as possible to 0.01, and the maximum acceptance is about 0.1)

      (3) Add 1-2μ and wipe it clean with a paper towel after determine

      (4) Add more samples

      (5) End of use: save data - unplug the U disk - shut down

      2. ClonExpress

      (1) AP-A and T7RNA polymerase gene

      (2) Calculate the amount of sample according to the instructions and the determined concentration, according to the instructions with the system (fragment and vector 3.5μL, buffer plus 4μL, enzyme plus 2μL, water to make up to 20μ, add 7μL of water), first add buffer and then recombinant enzyme, pipetting and mixing, and finally briefly centrifuge

      (3) PCR instrument temperature control 37℃ reaction, after the end of the 4 ℃ heat preservation

      Result:

      1. Concentration up to standard (75-200)

      T7：156.31ng/μL，A260/280=1.9,A260/230=0.85

      Vector:289.40ng/μL，A260/280=1.93,A260/230=1.28
