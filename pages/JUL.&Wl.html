Member: H.T.Q, Y.Y.X

Experiment:

Toxicity verification and absorption value exploration experiments

(1) Shake BL21, add 300 μ to 3 mL of LB medium

(2) According to a certain concentration gradient: 0.2mM;0.4mM;0.6mM;0.8mM; 1mM; 1.6mM; 2.3mM

Add naringenin/liquiritigenin

(3) Incubate in a shaker at 37 °C

(4) Samples were taken at intervals of 30min, 2h, 2h, 4h, and 5h, and 500μ bacterial liquid were aspirated

(5) Zero with LB medium, determine OD600 of the bacterial solution, and record the data

(6) Centrifuge at 5,000 rpm and collect the supernatant into a new 1.5 mL centrifuge tube

(7) Add the supernatant to the 96-well plate and detect the absorbance at 315 nm, 325 nm, 335 nm respectively (set up two replicates)

(8) Analysis data: OD600 was compared to the 0mM group to explore the effect of naringenin/liquiritigenin on the growth of E. coli

The data corresponding to the samples in the 96-well plate were calculated according to the standard curve to obtain the concentration of naringenin/liquiritigenin contained in the supernatant. Compare with the initial value

Result:

Preliminary experiment to see if E.coli absorbs naringenin and liquiritigenin and if naringenin and liquiritigenin affect the activity of E.coli.
